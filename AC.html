Advanced Diploma in Personal Training

The Advanced Diploma in Personal Training course will give you an extensive portfolio of skills and all the expertise you need to ensure that your career in fitness gets off to the best possible start. You'll learn how to start and run your own business, and develop a successful career in the industry.

Ante and Postnatal Exercise

Ante and postnatal clients are the mothers of your community and they need your support to stay fit and healthy. This specialism will provide you with the expertise and confidence to deliver safe and effective exercise programmes for women who are pregnant or have recently had a baby.

Aqua Training

Aqua exercise is an effective format that continues to flourish in leisure facilities around the country. It is very popular with a wide variety of exercisers, as a result of its fun, low-impact approach. Instructors in this discipline are in demand wherever there is a pool.

Aqua Training Module

There is a high demand for Aqua Instructors as people are looking for new, fun ways to workout. This an ideal course for Studio and Gym Instructors if you’re looking to boost your income and become more employable. On this course, you’ll learn how to deliver fun and effective sessions for clients of all abilities, using different approaches, with or without music.

Business Principles

Beyond your technical knowledge, you’ll also need to promote yourself and your skills to potential customers. Gain some essential business know-how to develop a profitable and successful business. As well as defining your unique selling point, you’ll also have a working business plan which will form the bedrock to your success.

CORE Athletic Conditioning with Fitness Circle

The Fitness Circle® resistance ring lives up to its name by providing peripheral resistance to a fitness-based workout.

COREtm Distance Running Workshop, Level 1

This workshop applies the CORE™ principles and guides a distance runner through movements to improve performance by increasing efficiency. Hip and pelvic stability is tested throughout the running motion, and provides a foundation of core stability and strength from which power and endurance can be developed.

COREtm Functional Strength and Resistance Training

By incorporating the CORE Foundational Principles, this workshop teaches you to use resistance training to improve functional strength. You will learn techniques that will prevent injuries and help you to reach your best performance. Resistance training is an essential part of athletic conditioning and is used in most sports to maximise athletes’ potential.

COREtm Instructor Foundation Course Level 1

The CORE™ Instructor Foundation Course Level 1 will introduce you to important concepts of athletic conditioning and performance training, with an emphasis on foundational principles. This course will cover alignment, biomechanics for the major joints and how to train the three classifications of muscles for optimal performance.
