About this course

The Advanced Diploma in Personal Training will provide youwith skills you need to make a difference in people's lives.

Our personal trainer courses are all delivered with face-to-face training to give you the practical experience of communicating, assessing and motivating your clients. We believe that training you in a gym and studio environment, with tutors and peers, will give you the right skills and exclusiveperience to start your PT career.

Take away

  • 30 days of face-to-face tuition, guidance and support from our experienced and industry qualified tutors
  • Get the complete package and learn the skills to train clients and small groups in Kettlebells, Padwork and Suspension Fitness
  • Gain the confidence to respond to client injuries and casualty situations with the Emergency First Aid CPD
  • Be able to start-up, develop and market your PT business with our business skills course units
  • Take part in inspiring practical workshops and engaging theory sessions to learn the skills and knowledge needed to build a longstanding career

Whether you are embarking on a new career as a qualified personal trainer, or looking to expand your professional fitness and health offering you will find this course can stand alone or compliment your current skills.

Course details

The course is delivered full-time with face-to-face tuition at our locations around the UK.

  • 23 days (Level 2 Gym Instructing and Level 3 Personal Training)
  • 3 days of practical assessments
  • 4 days of CPD courses: Padwork for Small Groups, Emergency First Aid, Suspension Training and Kettlebells Pro

If you are looking for a PT course that is part-time we also deliver the Level 3 Gym Instructing and Personal Training (Practitioner) nationwide.

What you will learn

Theory modules

  • Applied Anatomy and Physiology (Level 2 and 3)
  • Nutrition (Level 2 and 3)

Practical modules

  • Gym Instructing
  • Level 3 Personal Training
  • Padwork for Small Groups
  • Emergency First Aid
  • Suspension Fitness
  • Kettlebells Pro

Develop your skills to help clients achieve their goals

  • Learn how to customise and provide tailored gym and exercise programmes
  • Deliver one-to-one consultations and small group inductions to support clients in achieving their goals
  • Gain the skills needed to train a wide variety of individuals (including those with medical conditions which effect their lifestyle and wellbeing)
  • Gain an understanding of the role digital technology can play in supporting your clients programmes
  • Use your nutritional knowledge to support your client’s physical activity and help them achieve their personal goal

Develop your business skills and client base

  • Learning how to promote, market and sell your PT service
  • Understand how digital and social platforms can be used to grow your client base.

Develop your communication skills

  • Learn how to a communicate with clients effectively to promote wellness and exercise
  • Understand how rapport and soft skills can be used to motivate and change your clients behaviour

Be more versatile and employable

  • Plan effective sessions in Suspension Fitness, Kettlebells and Padwork training
  • Develop combat style sessions in Padwork for individuals and small groups
  • Train as a first-aider to manage casualties, minor burns and scalds (the Emergency in First Aid CPD is an essential qualification if you are going to work self-employed as it is required to gain liability insurance).
