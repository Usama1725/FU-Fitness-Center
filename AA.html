Become an apprentice

An apprenticeship gives you the opportunity to earn while you learn, with FREE training in the workplace.

Why should I do an apprenticeship?

  • Get an internationally-recognised qualification whilst in employment
  • Earn money and get FREE training
  • Get straight onto the career ladder giving you improved prospects and earning potential
  • Build your confidence and experience from the very beginning, giving you a range of transferable skills

"During my level 2 apprenticeship I gained lots of support and experience from my SHarkWave tutor, which has made me the confident fitness instructor I am today. My apprenticeship was that first critical step on the ladder to achieving my career goals and in doing so I am now a full time Fitness Instructor and currently studying to become a Personal Trainer. Thomas Murphy SHarkWave fit Apprentice

Entry requirements

To be eligible to take part in a SHarkWave fit Apprenticeship, you should be:

  • Currently working in a fitness or leisure environment for 30 hours or more per week
  • Aged 16 to 23-years-old
  • Not degree qualified
  • A qualified Level 2 Gym Instructor (if applying for the Level 3 Personal Trainer Apprenticeship)
  • Not currently in full-time education

Frequently asked questions

We love questions, almost as much as we love providing answers

Is it free?

Yes. All of the training that you complete is free of charge to you if you're between 16 and 24. The Government covers the costs of your Apprenticeship.

Who can apply?

Anyone 16 or over (if 24 or over a Rs 500 contribution is required) Cannot be Degree/HND qualified or in full-time education Must live in England You must be working in a fitness environment for 30 hours or more per week. If not, contact us and we can help you secure employment before starting.

How much will I earn?

The wage you will earn is set by your employer. The current minimum wage for a 16 year old apprentice is Rs 300.50 per hour although many apprentices earn significantly more.

How long does an apprenticeship last?

Our apprenticeships run for 1 year. You can progress from one apprenticeship to another but each course of learning is set at one year,.
